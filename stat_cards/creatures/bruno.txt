Bruno, the Rampaging Bear
Type: Large Beast, Unaligned
Challenge Rating: 4 (1,100 XP)
Role: Borderline deadly threat and Bjorn’s fierce enforcer

Core Stats
Armor Class: 14 (Natural Armor)
Hit Points: 95 (10d10 + 40)
Speed: 40 ft.
Initiative: +2

Ability Scores
Strength: 21 (+5)
Dexterity: 14 (+2)
Constitution: 18 (+4)
Intelligence: 2 (–4)
Wisdom: 12 (+1)
Charisma: 7 (–2)

Other Attributes
Saving Throws: —
Skills: Perception +3
Senses: Passive Perception 13
Languages: —


Attacks & Actions
Multiattack:
 Bruno makes two attacks: one with his bite and one with his claws.
Bite:
 Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
 Hit: 14 (2d8 + 5) piercing damage.
Claw:
 Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
 Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage.

Special Abilities
Rampage:
 When Bruno reduces a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack on his turn, he can move up to half his speed and make one bite attack as a bonus action.

Legendary Actions (Optional):
 Bruno can take 1 legendary action at the end of another creature’s turn. He can choose from the following options (only one option can be used at a time):
  • Swipe: Make one claw attack.
  • Stomp (Costs 2 Legendary Actions): Move up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks and make one bite attack.
