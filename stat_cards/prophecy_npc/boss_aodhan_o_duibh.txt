Actions
Multiattack: Aodhan casts a cantrip and makes one attack with his Dagger of Sorrow.

Dagger of Sorrow: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage plus 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.


Reap the Arcane (Recharge 5–6): Aodhan unleashes necrotic energy in a 20-foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 36 (8d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Aodhan regains hit points equal to half the total damage dealt.
Legendary Actions
Aodhan can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Aodhan regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.
Move: Aodhan moves up to his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
